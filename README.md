## 🎯 What is Revize?

Revize is an intelligent learning platform that helps users retain information using **spaced repetition + AI**.

Upload your study material (PDFs, images, notes, or links), and Revize automatically generates **flashcards** and schedules reviews at the right time.

👉 No manual flashcard creation  
👉 No forgetting what you studied  
👉 Smart reminders for optimal learning  

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🤖 AI-Powered Flashcard Generation
- Upload **PDFs / Word docs** (up to 3 pages)
- Upload **images / handwritten notes** (up to 10 images)
- Paste **text or links**
- AI extracts key concepts and generates **4–10 flashcards automatically**

---

- Day 9+ → Long-term retention

## 🧪 Why Spaced Repetition?

Without review:
- ❌ 50% forgotten in 24 hours

With Revize:
